Deals Local for Indirapuram

This is hyperlocal and ultra fast app for Indirapuram residents.

As Regular User:

  • View Promotions/Deals
  • Search Businesses
  • View all business in particular category
  • Share App with friends
  • Email suggestion in App

As Business Owner:

  • Verification using Google Id
  • List your business here
  • Publish deals in your business
  • Share this app with your customers
  • Email suggestion in App

Privacy Policy: Business data will be saved on servers. Some of the data is also saved on local device, to enable business search in absence of internet. You may wish to Sync for Data Update on desired interval to get any updates from server.
